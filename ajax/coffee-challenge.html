Maven Coffee Challenge Project

Project Info:

This is an in progress project. A group of investors looking to break into the US coffee market would like to leverage insights from "The Great American Coffee Taste Test." The investors asked for an explanatory report providing a data-driven strategy for opening their first coffee shop.

Project Details:

  • Technologies:Anaconda, Jupyter Notebook, Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Numpy, Seaborn, Tableau, Github
  • GitHub:github.com/davidblackwelder/maven-coffee-challenge
