Medical Appointment Project

Project Info:

This was my first project where I had to investigate a dataset. The dataset had information from 100K medical appointments in Brazil that focused on whether the patient showed up for their appointment or were a no-show for their appointment. All of my analysis is saved in a Jupyter Notebook.

Project Details:

  • Technologies:Anaconda, Jupyter Notebook, Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Numpy, Seaborn, Github
